## Business Objective
Help hiring managers, recruiters, and job seekers quickly understand:
- Average salaries by role, experience level, and location
- Salary differences: Remote vs On-site
- Top-paying companies and industries
- Year-on-year salary growth trends

## Key Features & KPIs
- **Filters**: Job Title, Experience Level, Employment Type (FT/Contract), Company Location, Remote/On-site
- **Visualizations**:
  - Average Salary by Job Title (Bar Chart)
  - Salary Distribution by Experience Level (Box Plot)
  - Remote vs In-Office Salary Comparison
  - Top 10 Highest-Paying Companies
  - Salary Trends by Country (Map + Heatmap)
  - YoY Salary Growth (Line Chart)
- **Interactive Elements**: Tooltips, drill-downs, highlight actions, dynamic titles
